Automated Drying Machine for Cacao Beans

The test results indicate the prototype can dry the cacao beans within 9 hours compared to the traditional sun drying that takes up to 30 hours of drying.

Joystick Controlled Surveillance Cam

Our project, "Joystick Controlled Surveillance Cam," leverages the power of Arduino technology to provide a cost-effective and efficient solution for enhanced airport security. This innovative system allows security personnel to manually control a surveillance camera using a joystick, providing real-time, flexible monitoring of critical areas. Our project not only aims to improve situational awareness and reaction times but also demonstrates the practical integration of hardware and software to address real-world challenges. By harnessing the capabilities of Arduino and mobile technology, we strive to deliver an adaptable, user-friendly surveillance solution tailored for the dynamic environment of an airport.
